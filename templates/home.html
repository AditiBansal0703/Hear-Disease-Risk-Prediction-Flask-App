Welcome to HeartCare

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide.

It occurs when the blood vessels that supply the heart become narrowed or blocked.

This Machine Learning model can predict the likelihood of heart disease based on several health factors.

Why early prediction is important?

  • 🧠 Early detection = Better treatment options!
  • 💓 Lifestyle changes can reverse risk factors.
  • 🏥 Regular monitoring reduces fatal outcomes.
